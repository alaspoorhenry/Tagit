Welcome to Tagit . We provide a simple solution for creating

digital tags for you belongings. To learn more, scroll down !

Create A Tag Return A Lost Item

STEP 1

Create a digital QR tag by providing your information in
the form below.

STEP 1

Scan the QR Code by using a QR scanning app or click on
the button below to scan it on this site.

STEP 2


Click on the print sticker button to print QR stickers containing
your contact information.

STEP 2

Upon scanning the QR code, follow the prompt to contact the
item's owner and return the item.
Thanks for being a good samaritan !

STEP 3


Stick the QR stickers to your belongings and if you are to lose
them in the future, the QR sticker provides an easy way for
people to contact you incase they found your items.
